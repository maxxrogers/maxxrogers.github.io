My Youth

Sport has played a pivotal role in shaping the person I am today. My experiences in playing representative AFL for the GWS Giants, as well as competing at high levels in cricket and soccer, have taught me invaluable lessons in discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Each sport has challenged me to push my limits, both physically and mentally, and has instilled a strong work ethic and determination to succeed. Now, as I participate in golf competitions, I continue to refine these qualities, learning the importance of patience and focus. Through sport, I've developed a well-rounded perspective on life, understanding the significance of perseverance, leadership, and maintaining a balanced mindset, all of which I carry with me in every aspect of my life.
