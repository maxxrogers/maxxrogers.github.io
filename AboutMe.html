About Me

I'm passionate about both teaching and being a barista, blending education with creativity in everything I do. As a teacher, I love inspiring my students and watching them grow. My barista skills allow me to bring a bit of artistry into everyday life, whether it's crafting the perfect cup of coffee or connecting with people over a shared love of great brews. In my free time, I'm all about sports—soccer, cricket, and golf keep me active and fuel my competitive side. I also have a love for the outdoors, especially when it comes to 4WD driving. There's nothing quite like the thrill of exploring off-road trails and tackling rugged terrains. Farming is another passion of mine; I find real satisfaction in working with the land and nurturing growth. Whether it's on the field or in the classroom, I thrive on challenge and adventure.

Further information About me

I'm passionate about cooking and love experimenting with new recipes in the kitchen. Working with timber is another hobby of mine, where I enjoy creating unique projects. I’m also a collector, with a particular interest in shoes and watches. I finished school in 2020 and was fortunate to receive a scholarship for university, which really opened up a lot of opportunities for me. .

My journey at Australian Catholic University (ACU), which began in 2021 and will conclude in mid-2025, has been transformative in shaping the person I am today. Throughout my time here, I've immersed myself in a diverse array of subjects, including textiles, woodwork, metal, food technology, computing, and education, among others. Each discipline has not only expanded my technical skills but also deepened my understanding of creativity, problem-solving, and teaching. ACU has provided me with a well-rounded education that has honed my ability to think critically, work with my hands, and inspire others. As I approach the end of this chapter, I am grateful for the experiences that have prepared me to pursue my passions with confidence and purpose.
